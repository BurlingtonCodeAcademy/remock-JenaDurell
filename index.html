ALL ABOUT CARTER

*HE IS A GOOD BOY*

TAKE A LOOK AT HOW CUTE HE IS!

"Dogs are our link to paradise." ~Milan Kundera

I DON'T WANT TO SAY I'M THE CUTEST DOG EVER...
BUT I'M THE CUTEST DOG EVER.

TOTALLY.

Carter plays with his chicken on the kitchen floor. The chicken, while beloved, was only with us for a very short time. Carter chewed through it in a week which was fine, because he has plenty of other toys. Some of them cause him great consternation- in the past the biggest problem was a stuffed squirrel, and currently he has a fox head with a rope string body that is somehow truly upsetting, but he loves it.
